Remarks
 

A conversion defines a relationship between two Amounts such that one amount ("From") is equivalent to another amount ("To") after being scaled by ConversionFactor. The resulting equation is:

Copy 
From * ConversionFactor == To
.

<example> var grams = new Amount(Gram, 1000.0); var kilograms = new Amount(Kilogram, 1.0); var conversion = new Conversion(grams, kilograms); </example>
