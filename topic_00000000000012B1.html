Food Class

Namespace: Esha.DomainModel
Assembly: Esha.DomainModel (in Esha.DomainModel.dll)
Represents a food substance, either simple or compound, measured in a specific amount and able to be analyzed for any Amount for which a Conversion exists in ConversionMap.
