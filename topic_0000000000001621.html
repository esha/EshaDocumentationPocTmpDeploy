ListenerSubscriptions Class

Namespace: Esha.DomainModel
Assembly: Esha.DomainModel (in Esha.DomainModel.dll)
Keeps track of subscribed IObservable<FoodVisitation> instances to a IObservable<FoodVisitation>, composes all subscriptions (instances of IDisposable returned from IObservable<T>.Subscribe), and allows awaiting of all listeners.
